What We Found

1. Retail Alpha Exists but Is Narrow

  • Concentrated in: sentiment/momentum, options selling, event-driven, and factor investing
  • HFT and pure stat arb are out of reach for retail
  • Simple strategies with good risk management beat complex models

2. LLMs Are a Genuine Edge

  • Earnings call tone analysis, Fed statement parsing, narrative detection ‚Äî these are NEW capabilities
  • Academic research validates LLM-based trading agents (TradingAgents, FinGPT, Alpha-GPT)

3. Sentiment Data Has Real Alpha

  • WSB crowd-sourced annual picks returned +76% in 2025 (vs ~25% S&P 500)
  • Ticker mention frequency and sentiment polarity have short-term predictive power (academically validated)

Risk Considerations

Market Risks

  • Regime change: Strategies that work in bull markets may fail in bear markets ‚Üí regime detection mitigates
  • Black swan events: Circuit breakers and drawdown limits protect capital
  • Crowding: Popular retail strategies lose edge ‚Üí continuous signal efficacy monitoring

Technical Risks

  • LLM hallucination: Financial reasoning errors ‚Üí verification layers, human oversight
  • API failures: Broker/data API outages ‚Üí fallback sources, graceful degradation
  • Overfitting: Backtested results ‚â† live results ‚Üí walk-forward validation, paper trading

Operational Risks

  • PDT rule: Need $25K+ for frequent day trading (or use swing trading)
  • Tax complexity: Short-term gains taxed as ordinary income ‚Üí consider tax-loss harvesting
  • Regulatory: No special registration needed for personal algo trading

Mitigation

  • Paper first, always: Minimum 30 days paper before any live capital
  • Small start: $5K-$10K maximum initial allocation
  • Hard limits: Non-overridable risk controls (15% max drawdown halts everything)
  • Human in the loop: All live trades require confirmation initially
  • Continuous monitoring: Daily reports, drawdown alerts, signal efficacy tracking

Final Recommendation

Build it. The research conclusively shows that an LLM-powered trading agent has a defensible edge in areas where contextual reasoning matters ‚Äî earnings analysis, sentiment synthesis, and multi-signal conviction. The tools are mature, the data is available, and the architecture is clear.

Start with the earnings event-driven strategy (highest edge, most AI-native) and sentiment momentum (most data available, academically validated). Add investor following for easy wins. Paper trade for 30 days minimum before committing real capital.

The goal is not to beat Renaissance Technologies. The goal is to beat buying and holding VTI ‚Äî systematically, with managed risk, and with an edge that grows as the AI agent learns what works.
