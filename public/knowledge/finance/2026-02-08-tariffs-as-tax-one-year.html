Tariffs = Hidden Consumption Tax

Many think tariffs are "paid by foreigners." They're not.

How tariffs flow:

  1. US importer buys goods from China, pays tariff to US Customs
  2. Importer passes cost to product prices
  3. Consumers pay more at Walmart/Amazon

Tariffs are essentially a consumption tax â€” hidden in every product you buy.

Why Tariffs Hurt GDP

  • Consumer purchasing power drops â†’ less spending
  • Business input costs rise â†’ lower profits â†’ less investment
  • Tax Foundation estimate: GDP down 0.5%
