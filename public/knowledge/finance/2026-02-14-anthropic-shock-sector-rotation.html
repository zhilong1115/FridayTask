What is Sector Rotation?

Sector rotation is when money flows from one industry sector to another. Not all stocks rise and fall together â€” money simply moves to a new home.

This week was a textbook case:

Money Out (Risk-Off)Money In (Defensive)
Tech / SaaS / SemiconductorsUtilities
Growth stocksConsumer Staples
High Beta stocksHealthcare
CryptoTreasuries

Why does rotation happen?

  1. Valuation reset: When tech P/E ratios are sky-high, any narrative crack sends money fleeing
  2. Rate sensitivity: Long-duration growth stocks (heavy future cash flows) are most rate-sensitive
  3. Risk aversion: Uncertainty drives money into low-volatility, high-dividend defensive sectors
