Professional investors employ a strategy called merger arbitrage:

  1. Buy target stock after announcement (e.g., SLAB @ $203)
  2. Wait for deal completion, receive offer price ($228)
  3. Pocket the spread (~12% return)

Sounds easy? But you bear the risk of deal failure. These funds are called Event-Driven Hedge Funds.
