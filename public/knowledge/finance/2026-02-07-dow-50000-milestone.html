What Makes the Dow Special

  • Only 30 stocks â€” Represents America's largest, most stable companies
  • Price-weighted â€” Higher stock price = more influence (not market cap!)
  • Oldest index â€” Founded 1896, over 125 years old
  • "Blue chips" â€” Industry leaders across sectors

Price-Weighted vs Market-Cap Weighted

This is a key distinction!

Dow (price-weighted): A 1% move in a $500 stock matters more than a 1% move in a $50 stock.

S&P 500 (market-cap weighted): A 1% move in a $3T company matters more than a 1% move in a $30B company.

So in the Dow, UnitedHealth ($510) has 5x more influence than Walmart ($108), even though Walmart has higher market cap!
