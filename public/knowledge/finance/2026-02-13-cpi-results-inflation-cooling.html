The October Shutdown Caveat:

The government shutdown in Oct 2025 meant BLS had to "carry forward" prices (assume no change) for many items. This creates a downward bias in YoY comparisons throughout 2026. The real inflation rate may be slightly higher than reported.

Market Reaction:

  • Stock futures: little changed (already priced in somewhat)

Tariff Impact Update:
